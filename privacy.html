Privacy Policy

Last update: December 16, 2022

What data K-Protect collects?

  • Servers and users IDs
  • Warned members IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Messages are not collected, but can be analysed if AntiFlood system is enabled
  • Channel settings, roles, basic server information, backup passwords (more details in "k.backup")
To delete your data, simply contact the support server. Otherwise, just wait exactly 30 days


Why does K-Protect collect this data?

  • To save K-Protect Bot's configuration files
  • To save all moderation changes in every server before they get changed
  • For Tracking Information
  • In order to record your action in case of a violation of the K-Protect Terms of Use
